About me

My name is Signija and I help women in Bournemouth area feel more confident. I focus on a sensitive approach, high-quality brands and education in simple, sustainable skincare. I speak English and Latvian.

I love my job and life in all its ways. I will always greet you with smile, I will put your favourite music on while I’ll be working on your beauty. I warm you with cosy atmosphere, great conversation and dose of humour.
